Bacon and Cheese Burger

a Lovely breakfast burger containing bacon, onions and cheese inside a pure beef patty. Perfect for serving with your favourite French fries.

Ingredients

  • 3 pounds ground beef
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 3 tablespoons barbeque sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 pound bacon,cut into 1/4 inch pieces
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 3/4 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 6 hamburgers buns, split

Steps

  1. Mix together the ground beef, salt, black pepper, barbeque sauce and garlic powder with your hands in a mixing bowl.
  2. Take a handfull of the mixture and shape into 12 patties.
  3. Place the bacon in a skillet and cook for about 5 minutes.
  4. When the bacon becomes evenly brown, remove the bacon and place them on papertowel to drain.
  5. Now fry the onions in the same skillet with the remaining bacon drippings.
  6. Mix the onions and bacon together in a small bowl.
  7. Prepare a large skillet on meduim heat.
  8. While the grill heats, take 6 patties and top them 1 1/2 tablespoons of the onion and bacon mixture and sprinkle with shredded cheese.
  9. Top each with one of the remaining patties and press the edges together to seal.
  10. Grill or pan fry the stuffed and sealed patties until cooked through, 2 to 3 minutes per side.
  11. Serve on hamburger buns with condiments of your choice.
