Baked Potato

Step by Step recipe for making a baked potato. Serve with your choice of toppings.

Ingredients

  • 1 - 6 baking potatos

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 175 degrees C (350 degrees F).
  2. Scrub the potato and prick it with a fork to prevent steam from building up and causing the potato to explode in your oven.
  3. Bake for 1 1/2 hours.
  4. Remove from oven and dress with your favourite topings.
