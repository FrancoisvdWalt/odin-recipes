Bacon and Cheese Burger

a Lovely breakfast burger containing bacon, onions on a pure beef patty smothered with a slice of cheese. Perfect for serving with your favourite French fries.

Ingredients

  • 3 pounds ground beef
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 3 tablespoons barbeque sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 pound bacon,cut into 1/4 inch pieces
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 3/4 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 6 hamburgers buns, split
