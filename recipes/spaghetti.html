Spaghetti

Dads Spaghetti

Quick and easy dish that can be made in 25 minutes, containing ingredients that can be found in any local store. With the standerd recipe you are able to serve 6 people comfortably.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • 2 pounds lean ground beef
  • 1 onion, minced
  • 1(6 ounce) can tamoto paste
  • 2(10.75 ounce) cans condensed tamoto soup
  • 2 and half cups water

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to boil.
  2. Add the pasta and boil for 8 to 10 minutes.
  3. After cooking drain water with strainer.
  4. In a large skillet over medium heat, cook ground beef with onion until meat is brown.
  5. Drain excess fluid from pan and add the tamoto paste,tamoto soup and water.
  6. Cook, stirring, until heated through and thickend.(5 to 10 minutes)
  7. Serve over cooked pasta.
