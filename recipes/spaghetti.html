Dads Spaghetti

Quick and easy dish that can be made in 25 minutes, containing ingredients that can be found in any store. With the standerd recipe you are able to serve 6 people comfortably.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • 2 pounds lean ground beef
  • 1 onion, minced
  • 1(6 ounce) can tamoto paste
  • 2(10.75 ounce) cans condensed tamoto soup
  • 2 and half cups water
